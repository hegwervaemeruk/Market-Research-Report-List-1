<p><strong>The Knife Mills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulley Knife Mills</li><li>Without Pulley Knife Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knife mills market is categorized into two main types: pulley knife mills and without pulley knife mills. </p><p>Pulley knife mills utilize a pulley system for driving the cutting blades, enabling efficient processing of various materials like plastics and wood. These mills are favored for their durability and versatility.</p><p>Without pulley knife mills, on the other hand, rely on direct drive motors for operation. They tend to be more compact and have fewer moving parts, offering ease of maintenance and suitability for small-scale applications.</p></p>
