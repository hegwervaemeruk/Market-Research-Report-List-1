<p><p>The cloth self-adhesive tape market is divided into two main types: uncoated and coated cloth self-adhesive tapes. Uncoated cloth tape is made from natural or synthetic fibers without any additional coating, providing a versatile and breathable option for various applications. In contrast, coated cloth self-adhesive tape features a layer of adhesive or protective coatings that enhance durability, moisture resistance, and adhesion strength. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and DIY projects for insulation, repairs, and bundling.</p></p>
