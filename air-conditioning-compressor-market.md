<p><ul><li>Piston Compressor</li><li>Rotary Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning compressor market consists primarily of two types: piston compressors and rotary compressors. Piston compressors utilize a reciprocating motion to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and versatility, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, rotary compressors use a rotating mechanism, providing a compact design, quieter operation, and smoother performance. Both types cater to different needs in HVAC systems, influencing the market dynamics based on performance, cost, and energy efficiency requirements.</p></p>
