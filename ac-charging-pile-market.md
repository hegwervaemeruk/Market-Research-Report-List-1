<p><p>The AC charging pile market is categorized mainly into Type I and Type II. Type I charging piles typically feature a single-phase power supply and are commonly used for residential applications, offering slower charging speeds. In contrast, Type II charging piles utilize a three-phase power supply, enabling faster charging and are often deployed in commercial and public charging stations. The distinction between these two types reflects their specific use cases, with Type II being more suitable for high-demand environments.</p></p>
