<p><p>The Food and Beverage Casters Market comprises two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating wheel assembly, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and ease of movement in tight spaces, making them ideal for mobile food carts and service equipment. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have fixed wheels that provide stability and support for heavy loads, suitable for stationary equipment like kitchen racks and storage units. Both types are crucial for optimizing efficiency in foodservice operations.</p></p>
