<p><p>Sun Control Window Film for automobiles is categorized into three main types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films provide UV protection without altering visibility, making them ideal for maintaining aesthetics. Dyed films utilize a color dye to absorb heat and reduce glare while also offering privacy. In contrast, Vacuum Coated films have a reflective surface that blocks heat and glare more effectively, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort within the vehicle. Each type serves distinct customer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
