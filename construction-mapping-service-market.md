<p><p>The Construction Mapping Service market is primarily divided into two types: Aerial Surveying and Terrestrial Surveying. Aerial Surveying involves the use of drones and aerial platforms to capture high-resolution imagery and data from above, enabling efficient large-scale site assessments. Both methods are essential for effective planning, monitoring, and managing construction projects, enhancing accuracy and reducing timeframes.</p></p>
