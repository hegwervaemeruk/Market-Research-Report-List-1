<p><p>Machine Condition Monitoring (MCM) systems are categorized into three main types: Online Machine Monitoring, Portable Machine Monitoring, and Others. Online Machine Monitoring involves continuous, real-time data collection and analysis using permanently installed sensors, enabling immediate detection of faults. Portable Machine Monitoring utilizes handheld devices, allowing technicians to assess machine health at various locations and times, making it flexible and adaptable. The "Others" category encompasses hybrid solutions or specific applications tailored to unique industrial needs, enhancing overall asset management.</p></p>
