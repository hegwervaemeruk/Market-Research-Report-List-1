<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Floor Type Projector market includes three primary technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS). DLP projectors utilize micro mirrors for light projection, known for sharp images and color accuracy. LCD projectors use liquid crystal panels, offering bright and vivid displays ideal for presentations. LCoS combines elements of both, using liquid crystals on silicon for high-resolution images and superior contrast. Each type caters to distinct preferences and applications in various settings.</p></p>
